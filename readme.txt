== Description ==
Have a Now page on [OurNows](https://ournows.com)? Embed your Now page in your website.

Your Now is an evolving overview of what you are working on, what keeps you entertained, healthy, and productive, and what kind of lifestyle, rituals, and practices you adopt. You can think of it as a public journal, diary, or logbook at 10,000 feet.

Record and reflect on your Now, observe Nows of your friends and other fascinating people, and inspire your next.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/ournows` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3.Include the following code in your page on Wordpress:

	[ournows username="Your_Username"]


For example if your username on [OurNows](https://ournows.com) is John, include:

	[ournows username="john"]
